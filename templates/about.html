Our Vision

Redefining Hourly Staffing

SHIFTLY is on a mission to connect local student workers with nearby business opportunities instantly. By replacing traditional application processes with intuitive swipe matches, we make working flexible and staffing stress-free.

Why SHIFTLY?

Traditional job applications are slow and outdated. Students need quick gigs to support their education, and businesses face sudden shift shortages. SHIFTLY fills this gap by introducing a Tinder-style matching platform built on speed, trust, and transparency.

With upfront escrow financing, verified credential badges, and double-blind rating score systems, both parties are fully protected and motivated to maintain top-tier professionalism.

Our Commitments

  • Punctual Payouts: Escrow deposits guarantee weekly bank releases.
  • Rigorous Moderation: Shifts and companies are vetted by system administrators.
  • Aesthetic Simplicity: Simple gestures, responsive dashboard metrics, and built-in chat.
